people COMPANY

The Company committee handles the external relations for Hackers@VT. They coordinate with company representatives for hosting events on campus (such as information events, professional career prep, coding challenges, etc.). As one of the goals of Hackers@VT is to bring student's closer to the professional applications of programming, company relationships are an important part of our activity, and each company representative will have direct contact with a member of our Company committee.

If your company is interested in partnering with Hackers@VT, you can contact us here.

code HACKATHON

The Hackathon committee works with connecting Virginia Tech students to hackathons across the nation, and running internal hackathons at Virginia Tech (such as Build to Learn). Hackathon committee members find hackathons hosted by other universities which will be empowering experiences for students, and then communicate with the hackathon organizers for providing free transportation to all attendees. Each semester, Hackers@VT finds 3-4 hackathons for Virginia Tech students to coordinate for students.

Committee members also plan and run internal hackathons (open to Virginia Tech students only) - including organizing dates, location, food, prizes, and all other logistic aspects - to give students a great introduction to the hackathon experience.
